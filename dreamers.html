The Dreamers Project is a Covenant University Initiative that was birthed by the Vice Chancellor of Covenant University in 2015.

In a chapel service, the Vice Chancellor shared his dreams of building world class business and technologies that will create impact right here from the University and he made a call to his fellow dreamers; the students and staff encouraging anyone with business ideas or products to share their dreams.

Over a hundred ideas were submitted but only a few could be chosen.

This website is a living exhibition of the Covenant University Dreamers. It is a repository of all dreamer projects and what they are all about.

The business and products birthed from the Dreamers Initiative cuts across major industries such as Education, Health, Transportation, Food, Lifestyle, Agriculture, Security and New Technology and Mobile Money.

Mobile Waiter

Mobile Waiter is a food delivery startup that operates in Covenant University and it is the cheapest way to buy food. Customers can order for food from their mobile phones or computers and it is delivered in 30 minutes.

They cater to the needs of the busy professionals on campus by helping them get their food from various selling outlets and also items from the shopping mall.

Mobile Waiter also serves as a bridge between the school campus and external restaurants as customers can make orders from popular brands such as KFC and Dominos.

Name of Dreamer(s): Hussaini Odunayo and Douglas K. Ayeba

Adenanthera Pavonina Linn Seed

The Adenanthera Pavonina Linn Seed is a seed that can be seen on the trees in the environment of the University Campus and even in Canaanland. Its available almost throughout the year

These dreamers were able to carry out research on the seed and they discovered that it had many uses.

After the necessary and quality tests, the seeds were found to be suitable for its intended use without causing harm. The seeds can be eaten but only after they have been roasted and after due processing can be used as vegetable oil.

The Adenanthera Pavonina Linn Seed can also be used to produce poultry feed such as Broiler Finisher Mash, Broiler Starter Mash, Chick Mash, Layer Mash.

Name of Dreamer(s): Owoeye Taiwo Felicia & Akinlabu Kehinde Deborah

Unmanned Aerial Vehicles

One of the things the Vice Chancellor highlighted in his call to fellow dreamers was the need to start developing Unmanned Aerial Vehicles.

An Unmanned Aerial Vehicle popularly known as a drone is basically an aircraft in the sky that can be controlled remotely and carry out autonomous missions.

The Drone team in Covenant University has successfully built a number of drones equipped with HD Cameras and capable of autonomous flight.

Heart Monitoring Guard

A lot of peeple lose their lives as a result of diseases related to heart conditions.

One of the many reasons for this is that people do not know their heart problems and what to do whenever heart related issues come up.

The Heart Rate Guard is a device that senses cardiovascular problems and alerts contact in case of emergencies.

The product is in a product development phase.

Beaulah Edu

The percentage of failures in the WAEC and SSCE examinations is very high and has continued to increase in recent years. Proper preparation and access to knowledge can be identified as one of the key obstacles to turning the situation around and increasing the percentage of successes in the exams.

BeaulahEdu aims to solve that problem by providing answers to past questions of examinations like WAEC, JAMB and NECO.

The unique approach to BeaulahEdu's proposition is that the answers are presented in form of videos. So students can always rewatch videos until they are understood.

Drop.taxi is a PUDO (Pick Up Drop Off) service that aims to solve the problem of getting taxi services to and from to airport to various destinations.

The service is available on the Android platform and allows users book for their taxis via the app.

The app also affords users the opportunity to track the taxis they've paid for via the app.

Intercept

Intercept is a movie that is aimed at reinventing the future of African cinema and crossing the boundaries of filmmaking.

It is a a short film set in the year 2019. The movie explores how in a near future Nigeria's security body will harness modern technology to battle national challenges like terrorism and cybercrime.

Intercept's unique proposition is the use of CGI and VFX techniques to aid visual storytelling.
